1. Project Idea

App Description

This website helps high school students quickly find clubs and activities offered specifically at their high school. Students can search by category or interest and see important details like meeting times and locations. The app focuses on making it easier for high school students to get involved and discover new opportunities on school property.

Target Audience

This website is designed specifically for high school students who want to explore and join clubs, sports teams, or extracurricular activities at their high school. It helps students learn about what's available and how to participate, making it easier for everyone to connect with their interests and peers within their high school community.
